Our Story

Crafting Timepieces Since 1952

Founded by master clockmaker Alberto Moretti in Florence, Italy, Alberto Clocks has been creating exquisite timepieces for over six decades. What began as a small workshop crafting bespoke clocks for local nobility has grown into an internationally renowned brand.

Today, we continue Alberto's tradition of excellence, blending time-honored craftsmanship with modern precision engineering. Each clock is still assembled by hand in our Florence workshop, ensuring the highest quality and attention to detail.

Premium Materials

We source only the finest woods, metals, and mechanisms from around the world.

Precision Engineering

Each movement is tested for accuracy and reliability before leaving our workshop.

Sustainable Practices

We're committed to environmentally responsible manufacturing and sourcing.
